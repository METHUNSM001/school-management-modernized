A Legacy of Quality Education

Montfort School is located at D.G.Pudur Erode (Dt) on the Sathy – Athany main road. The school was started on **June 18, 2012** by the **MONTFORT BROTHERS of ST.GABRIEL – BENGALURU PROVINCE**.

What started as a vision to provide quality education in a natural atmosphere has grown into a premier educational institution with 663 students, studying from PRE-KG to XII std. Affiliated to the CBSE, our school has a dedicated band of 44 teaching and 11 non-teaching staff.

Principal's Message

Montfort School is a place where every child's journey is cherished. Our infrastructure, featuring vast playgrounds, smart classrooms, and advanced laboratories, is designed to cater to modern educational needs.

We emphasize not just academics but also physical and creative growth through Karate, Silambam, Music, and Dance. Our environment-friendly campus provides a natural atmosphere where learning becomes an enjoyable discovery.
